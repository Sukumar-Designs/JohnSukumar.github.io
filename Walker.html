Problem Statement

Understand the needs of elderly individuals

Create a product to aid in everyday life

Conducting User Research

Each person on the four person team conducted two interviews - for a total of eight

During my interviews I asked potential users what everyday problems were particularly annoying

Investigating How the Walker Can be Improved

A common theme amongst the elderly individuals we interviewed was a lack of customization in their lives

During my interview, my interviewee complained about difficulties using their walker, particularly how bulky it was
