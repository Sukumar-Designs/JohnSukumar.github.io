Problem Statement

  • Create a tangible product to commemorate college friendships at graduation

Background

Apartment Mates

My two roommates and I threw a series of parties at our apartment in our senior year of college as a way of deepening our relationships with friends and meeting new people

For each party, we decided on a theme, and I designed a poster

Initially I thought to create a t-shirt out of each poster. However, the cost was too high. To finalize the year, a crew neck sparked my interest and was a popular idea with my friends when I pitched it

Redoing the Apartment Logo

Original Apartment Logo

I made a logo, at the beginning of senior year, for our apartment as a fun way to brand ourselves

Two problems existed:

  • I made the original logo using Midjourney, and I wanted to use an original design
  • The logo was too complicated to be embroidered

To update the logo I chose to portray our fostered cat Doozer as he was a highlight of our shared experience

Design Choices:

  • Purple for Northwestern’s color
  • Blue as it complimented best with the black color I chose for the crew neck
  • JAM is an acronym created by taking the first letter of my and my apartment mates’ names

Finding a Vendor and Submitting an Order

Printful Creation Process

After much research, I chose Printful for ease of process and their embroidery. This was crucial as I wanted these crew necks to last

Final Steps:

  • Ask who wanted to purchase a crew neck
  • Make a spreadsheet with each order (n=13) including size and payment information
  • Make a bulk order
  • Distribute to everyone
