Problem Statement

Create a friendly envrionment for those new to powerlifting

Intuitive to use

Expand on current power rack functionality

Sketching Out My Initial Ideas

Body

I struggled with the design language of the power rack at first

It needed to be pleasant and playful - inviting people use the machine

Weights

I needed to match the weights to the rest of the body. I had two criteria for the weights:

  • Easy to interact with
  • Not distracting from body

Modeling and Rendering


Once I had the sketches laid out I modeled it in SolidWorks

Power Rack For Gym Novists

Interactable locations on the rack are colored a playful purple:

  • Twisting the top-middle knob, height adjusts the bar hooks
  • Weights are purple to invite the user to use them



I learned KeyShot in order to render the Power Partner aesthetically
